ingredients:jello instant banana pudding 2 boxes 8 oz.
            1 bunch of bananas
            1 box of nilla wafers 
            1 tub of cool whip original
prepare pudding as directed on box.slice bananas.line bottom of pan with nilla wafers.add some of slices of bananas on top of wafers and layer that with pudding mix and repaet till full.add cool whip on top.crush hand full of nilla wafers sprinkle on top with cool whip and let chill for 3 hours.
